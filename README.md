# 🍕 Pizza Sales Project | Power BI + SQL
A comprehensive data analysis project on Pizza Sales dataset to derive key business insights using SQL and Power BI.

## 📊 Project Objective
To analyze pizza sales data and provide actionable insights on revenue performance, customer ordering behavior, product popularity, and seasonal trends to help optimize business strategy.

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies
- **SQL Server** – Data Cleaning, Exploration & KPI Calculation
- **Power BI** – Interactive Dashboard & Visualization
- **DAX** – Advanced Calculations
- **Excel** – Initial Data Review

---

## 📌 Key Questions & KPIs Analyzed

### Overall Business Performance
- What is the **Total Revenue** generated?
- What is the **Average Order Value (AOV)**?
- How many **Total Pizzas** were sold?
- How many **Total Orders** were placed?
- What is the **Average Pizzas per Order**?

### Time-based Analysis
- Which **day of the week** has the highest number of orders?
- Which **month** has the highest number of orders?
- How does order volume vary across different months?

### Category & Size Performance
- Which **pizza category** generates the highest revenue and what is its contribution?
- Which **pizza size** is most popular and contributes the highest revenue?
- Performance of categories and sizes in specific periods (January & Q1)

### Top & Bottom Performers
- What are the **Top 5 best-selling pizzas** by revenue?
- What are the **Bottom 5 pizzas** by revenue?
- What are the **Top 5 pizzas** by quantity sold?
- What are the **Bottom 5 pizzas** by quantity sold?
- What are the **Top 5 most ordered pizzas**?
- What are the **Bottom 5 least ordered pizzas**?

---

## 🔍 Data Analysis Process

1. **Data Understanding & Cleaning**
   - Explored the raw pizza_sales dataset
   - Checked for missing values, duplicates, and data types
   - Standardized date formats and handled necessary conversions

2. **Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) using SQL**
   - Calculated core KPIs (Total Revenue, Total Orders, Total Pizzas Sold, etc.)
   - Performed time intelligence analysis (Day-wise & Month-wise trends)
   - Analyzed performance by pizza category and pizza size
   - Identified top and bottom performing pizzas by revenue, quantity, and order count

3. **Advanced Analysis**
   - Calculated percentage contribution of each category and size
   - Created period-specific analysis (January month & First Quarter)
   - Identified high-performing and under-performing products

4. **Visualization in Power BI**
   - Built interactive dashboards with slicers for Month, Quarter, and Category
   - Used KPI cards, bar charts, pie charts, line charts, and tables
   - Implemented drill-through and bookmarks for better user experience

---

## 📈 Key Project Insights

- The business generated significant revenue with a healthy **Average Order Value**.
- Certain pizza categories and sizes dominate the sales — indicating clear customer preferences.
- Order volume shows noticeable variation across days of the week and months.
- A small set of pizzas contribute to a large portion of total revenue and orders (Pareto principle observed).
- There is a clear gap between top-performing and bottom-performing pizzas, suggesting opportunities for menu optimization.
- January and Q1 analysis revealed important seasonal patterns that can guide promotional strategies.

---

## 🎯 Business Recommendations

- Focus marketing and inventory efforts on **Top 5 performing pizzas**.
- Review and improve strategies for **Bottom 5 pizzas** (possible price adjustment, bundling, or removal).
- Run targeted promotions on low-performing days/months to balance order volume.
- Prioritize **best-selling pizza sizes and categories** in procurement and menu planning.
- Use insights from category and size contribution to optimize pricing strategy.
